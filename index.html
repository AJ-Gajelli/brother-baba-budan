Our Story

The legend of the seven seeds

In the 15th century, the mystic Baba Budan secreted seven coffee seeds from Mocha to India — defying the Arab traders' monopoly and forever changing what the world woke up to. That same rebellious spirit lives in this café.

"An original in the Melbourne coffee scene, known for exceptional Seven Seeds coffee and the passion to serve it."

Step inside and you'll notice the chairs. Not on the floor — hanging from the ceiling, layered into a dense canopy of wood and metal above your head. It's not a gimmick. It's a statement: ordinary spaces can be made extraordinary with the same boldness it takes to smuggle seeds across a border.

Look up. A hundred chairs hang above you — the café's signature installation, turning the ceiling into a meditation on space, weight, and the art of turning a place upside down.

What we serve

Coffee with a point of view

Every cup pours from Seven Seeds' own roastery — direct-trade relationships, seasonal micro-releases, and an obsession with what's in the cup.

Espresso & Milk

Flat white, latte, cappuccino — all built on the Seven Seeds espresso blend. Roasted in-house, dialled in daily.

Filter & Pour-over

Single origin Hario V60 and batch brew, rotating weekly. Ask the barista what's on — the answer is always worth the question.

Seasonal Pastries

Baked by Suburbia. The croissant, coffee scroll, and rotating specials change with what's good — show up early, they go fast.

Beans & Merch

Take the roast home. Retail bags of the espresso blend, seasonal single-origins, and the occasional micro-release for those in the know.
